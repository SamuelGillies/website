Composition Catalogue | Solo Performer


There's fire where we're going (2019). For violin and electronics.
Premiered by Sarah Saviet at St Pauls Hall, Huddersfield, United Kingdom on March 04, 2019.

a sound world for small things (2019). For one performer with sound objects and electronics.
Premiered by Colin Frank at Phipps Recital Hall, Huddersfield, United Kingdom on August 17, 2019.

Myrkur Dagar (2016). For voice and electronics.
Premiered by Juliet Fraser at St Pauls Hall, Huddersfield, United Kingdom on February 09, 2017.

Dinner For One (2014). For tabletop percussion.
Premiered by Louise Devenish at 'Australian Music for One Percussionist' at Hackett Hall, University of Western Australia on December 05, 2014.

Snowden (Eyes in the Sky) (2014). For bass clarinet and electronics.
Premiered by Dr Linday Vickery at 'Improvising Across Borders' at Školská 28, Prague on July 20, 2014.

Snowden (2014). For electric guitar and electronics.
Premiered by Chris Kotchie at 'Club Zho #110' at The Bakery, Perth, Australia on August 19, 2014. Commissioned by TURA New Music.

The Place We Used To Hang Out Until We Disappeared I (2012). For piano and electronics.
Premiered by Fond Recollections Ensemble at West Australian Academy of Performing Arts on November 08, 2012.

The Times We Ran Back Into The Fire I (2012). For electric guitar and electronics.
Premiered by Fond Recollections Ensemble at West Australian Academy of Performing Arts on November 08, 2012.

Red River (2011). For bass drum, cymbal and electronics.
Premiered by James Chong at 'Breaking Out', 10th Totally Huge New Music Festival at Studio Underground, State Theatre of Western Australia on September 27, 2011.

Threshold (2011). For bass clarinet and electronics.
Premiered by Points of Departure Ensemble at West Australian Academy of Performing Arts on November 27, 2011.

Resistance (2011). For cello and electronics.
Premiered by Points of Departure Ensemble at West Australian Academy of Performing Arts on November 27, 2011.
