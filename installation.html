Composition Catalogue | Installation


infinitely gentle, infinitely suffering (2018). Speaker array and two-channel video projection.
Premiered at Huddersfield Contemporary Music Festival, 'SPIRAL Showcase', SPIRAL Studio, Huddersfield, UK on November 17-19, 2018.

Burning Embers (2013). Eight sound producing modules with suspended speakers.
Premiered at Roe Street Car Park, Perth, Australia on June 10-July 10, 2013. Commissioned by Foodchain as part of Transart 2013.

Extended States (2011). Stereo audio, video projection, and bespoke sculptures.
Premiered at Sound Spectrum Gallery, Perth, Australia on August 09, 2011.

Dying Embers (2010). Twelve defaced speakers and three car tape-decks.
Premiered at ECU Library Mount Lawley, Perth, Australia on June 15, 2010.

Glowing Embers (2010). Twelve defaced speakers and three car tape-decks.
Premiered at K.K. Gallery, ECU Mt Lawley, Perth, Australia on May 20, 2010.
